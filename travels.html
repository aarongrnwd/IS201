Mexico, the city of Torreón, Coahuila

Torreón, Coahuila is a city that lies in the northern region of Mexico. It is more of a hot and dry climate where having protection from the sun is a must. You can find many activities to do as well as eat some delicious, albeit very unique, food.

Overlooking the city of Torreón, Coahuila from on a mountain.

One of the many sites you can see is El Cristo de las Noas. A large statue of Christ with his arms spread wide in a welcoming manner that overlooks the city.

El Cristo de las Noas overlooking the city of Torreón, Coahuila.

Mexico has a great selection of delicious food to try. However, sometimes they will have strange ones to try aswell. While in Torreón, I had the opportunity to eat grasshoppers. Have a look at the picture below.

Grasshoppers are one of the many strange foods you can try while in Mexico.

London, England

On the streets of London, England at the Big Ben clock tower.

My next stop... London, England. My parents moved to London in 2022 and I had the opportunity to visit them. London is such a large and diverse place with people from all over the world. This creates an amazing atmosphere with so much to do.

Exploring what China Town had to offer in London, England. The streets of London, England at the London Towere Bridge.

One of the many stops included China Town where we explored the shops and ate great Chinese food. My mom even got to practice her Mandarin skills. Later on, we headed to the streets of London and made a stop at the famous London Tower Bridge.

Paris, France

During my time with my parents, we made a week trip to Paris using the train that goes through the Chunnel (a tunnel that goes underneath the English Channel). This trip included many stops at the iconic places including the Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, Sainte Chapelle, Sainte Chapelle, and Versailles just to name a few.

We made a visit to the famous Eiffel Tower during our stay. A stop at the famous Arc De Triomphe was of course included in our trip. The interiour of the Sainte Chapelle church in Paris, France is gorgeous.

France is well known for their culinary arts. We wouldn't be able to call it a trip to Paris without trying their well known escargot (snail as food). Don't think that eating something slimy you find in your yard is appealing? I wouldn't have thought so either. Surprisingly it is not that bad when they are warm and seasoned. Have a look below.
